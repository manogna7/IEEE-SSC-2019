About SSC'19

Redefining Technology In The New Millennium

The Congress is an annually held flagship event of IEEE Hyderabad Section, which is an amalgamation of Technical Talks, talks on soft skills and career opportunities, wherein student members of various Universities/ Colleges under the IEEE Hyderabad Section meet to learn, discuss and network. Last year SSC was organized for 2 days with more than 300 delegates from all the Student Branches and 50+ volunteers.

IEEE Hyderabad Section is one of the most active sections of IEEE in India. IEEE was formally introduced in Hyderabad, in June 1981, when a group of engineers in Hyderabad decided to form an IEEE sub-Section here. This sub-Section was rapidly elevated to a full Section, in 1984 (the year of IEEE Centenary celebrations)
